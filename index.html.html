Welcome to Rozen!

We are Rosette and Zee—two passionate female educators who have spent the past five years teaching and exploring the vibrant cultures of Asia. With teaching experience across Hong Kong, China, Indonesia, Malaysia, Thaialnd and Singapore, we’ve developed a deep love for the region and a shared mission: to help young learners grow confident in speaking English.

Through Rozan English Camps, we aim to create fun, immersive, and interactive experiences that go beyond the classroom. Our camps, held during the summer and winter holidays in Malaysia and Singapore, are designed to spark curiosity, build communication skills, and empower kids to express themselves in English with ease and joy

Meet Rosette

Rosette is a passionate and experienced English teacher with over five years of teaching across Asia, including Hong Kong, China, Malaysia, and Singapore. As a native English speaker, she brings both fluency and a fun, engaging teaching style to every classroom. Rosette has a natural connection with children and is known for her kind, patient, and enthusiastic approach. She truly loves working with kids and is dedicated to helping them grow in confidence and communication through English.

Meet Zee

Zee is a vibrant and passionate English teacher currently based in Thailand. With teaching experience in Indonesia and a strong background in conducting English camps across Malaysia, Singapore, and Thailand, Zee brings energy, creativity, and heart to every learning environment. Known for her youthful spirit and dynamic teaching style, she is deeply committed to making English fun, engaging, and confidence-boosting for every child she teaches.
